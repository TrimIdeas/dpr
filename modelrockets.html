There are diffrent rocket motors!

Setting up the rocket



know to launch the model rocket you will first put the motor of your chosing (I recomend a B, or C motor) in the bottem. Next you willstuff tisue paper in the tube to protect the parachute, the reson why you do this is because without the tisue paper the charge at the end of the motors life that pushes the parachute out would end up riping the parachute. Next you fold, not stuff, the parachute in to the tube along with the cords and rubberband, and put on the nose cone. now you will slide the rocket on to the launch stand then you will get the little meatal and plastic things that came with the motors and stick the metal thing in the hole in the bottem of the motor then use the plastic thing to plug the metal thing in to the motor. lastly you will clip the launching devices cords onto the meatal prongs.



Launching the rocket



To launch the rocket you will take the launching device which looks like a box with a pin, as far as it goes away from the rocket without disconecting the prongs from the clips. now take the pin and put it in the hole, and make sure the box has batterys in it then push the pin in slightly hard and if the green light shows then do a count down and push the button down. it will launch and it will climb and when it is at apogee it will start falling and the parachute will deploy carying the rocket safely to the ground. The rocket can be reused, but the motor cannot.
